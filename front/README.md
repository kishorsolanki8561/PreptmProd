## Usage

- **Primary Color (`#007BFF`)** is used for important elements like buttons, links, and highlights.
- **Secondary Colors** are used for less emphasized elements, backgrounds, or borders.
- **Text Colors** should be used to maintain readability and contrast based on the theme.
- **Link Colors** are for different states of links: default, hover, and visited.
- **Selected Color** is used to indicate selected items or active elements in navigation.
- **Un-selected Color** is used for items that are not currently selected or inactive.
